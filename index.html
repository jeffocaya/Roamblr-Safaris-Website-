Who We Are

From Solo Saturdays
to Safari Company

When Jeff Ocaya moved from Kitgum to Kampala, he started doing something simple: every Saturday, he'd follow a random road until he found something interesting.

Those solo micro-adventures — Rubaga Cathedral, Kabaka's Lake, Buziga Hill, the shores of Lake Victoria — weren't a business plan. They were just curiosity. But when friends saw his photos and started asking "where should I go?" a business was born.

Roamblr Safaris is the professional answer to that question. We're a Kampala-based ground handling company serving international tour agencies, Ugandan families, honeymooners, school groups, and corporate teams.
